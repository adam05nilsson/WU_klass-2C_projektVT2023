Privat Coaching

We will assing you one of our coaches that will help you improve your communication skills and become a better public speaker with our 6-week coaching program. During the first week, He or her will asses your current abilities and create a customized coaching plan to fit your specific needs. For the next four weeks, you'll have regular coaching sessions where your coach will focus on different aspects of communication, such as body language, voice modulation, storytelling, and persuasion. You'll also have assignments to complete on your own to reinforce your skills. In the final week, your coach will evaluate your progress and provide recommendations for further improvement.

Company Coaching

Our personalized coaching includes an initial assessment and customized coaching plan, focusing on aspects such as body language, voice modulation, storytelling, and persuasion. Your team will complete assignments to reinforce their new skills, and at the end of the program, we'll evaluate their progress and provide recommendations.
